PR, writing, and storytelling for contemporary art
Seoyeon Choi is a PR professional and writer based in Seoul, working between galleries, artists, and institutions.
She develops press materials, exhibition texts, and communication strategies for contemporary art, connecting local narratives with international audiences through clear, precise language.
Selected work includes collaborations with museums, art fairs, and publishers on press releases, interviews, catalogs, and editorial features.
